Projects

Project 1: Summit Pothole Challenge

I created a 2D arcade game for my final project for my Creative Coding class
using JavaScript and p5JS, a client-side library. Through this library, I was able to
easily connect a gaming controller and map the button and joystick inputs to the code.

          

Project 2: Impact of AI in Communications

A Strategic Communications major and I researched the effects of AI in the
Communications field and created a survey with an article that was written
once by us and once again by AI (ChatGPT).
Participants were asked to distinguish the human article from the AI one.

          

Project 3: Coffee Kiosk using classes in Java

As a way to practice using classes in Java, I was asked to make a mini coffee kiosk
that allowed users to order and customize their drinks with many cup sizes and flavor shots.
Every option had a price, meaning that users had a total cost that they could see
when finalizing the order.
